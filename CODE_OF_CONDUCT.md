# 社区行为准则 / Community Code of Conduct

## 中文

ZhuàngTài 社区致力于提供开放、友善且无骚扰的协作环境，不因年龄、身体状况、族裔、性别认同与表达、经验水平、教育背景、社会经济地位、国籍、外貌、种族、宗教或性取向而区别对待。

我们鼓励：尊重不同观点；提供具体、建设性的反馈；承认错误并愿意修正；把社区整体利益置于个人胜负之上。

不可接受的行为包括：侮辱或贬损性言论；人身攻击；公开或私下骚扰；未经许可发布他人的私人信息；以及在专业协作环境中不适当的其他行为。

维护者有权删除、编辑或拒绝不符合本准则的评论、提交、代码、Issue 与其他贡献，并可临时或永久限制参与。请通过 GitHub 的私密安全报告渠道联系维护者。所有报告都会在合理范围内保密处理。

本准则适用于项目空间，也适用于代表本项目参与公开活动的场景。

## English

The ZhuàngTài community is committed to an open, welcoming, and harassment-free collaboration environment, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socioeconomic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We encourage respect for differing viewpoints, specific and constructive feedback, acknowledging and correcting mistakes, and prioritizing the health of the community over individual advantage.

Unacceptable behavior includes insulting or derogatory comments, personal attacks, public or private harassment, publishing another person's private information without permission, and other conduct inappropriate in a professional collaboration environment.

Maintainers may remove, edit, or reject comments, commits, code, issues, and other contributions that do not align with this code, and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation. Contact the maintainers through GitHub's private security reporting channel. Reports will be handled confidentially to the extent reasonably possible.

This code applies within project spaces and when someone is publicly representing the project.
